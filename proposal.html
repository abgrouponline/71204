Cottsway Digital Delivery

Responsive Software, Data, and Reporting Support

Modern managed service aligned to Cottsway’s Procurement Act notice 2025/S 000-071204.

Mission Impact

Deliver a flexible, always-on extension of Cottsway’s IT & Projects team. We will provide continuous software development, analytics, and reporting capacity across UK regions, ensuring every request is triaged rapidly, built with transparent pricing, and measured against clear SLAs.

Engagement Blueprint

1. Kick-off (Week 0–2)

  • Assign Account Lead + Technical Delivery Manager for single-touch accountability.
  • Run discovery workshops with IT, Projects, and key business users to map the current stack, data sources, and reporting commitments.
  • Co-create SLA catalogue covering incident tiers, change requests, planned releases, and analytics refresh cadence.

2. Service Mobilisation (Week 2–4)

  • Implement secure request intake (In-Tend messages mirrored into our shared Kanban board) with shared dashboards.
  • Stand up automation pipelines (CI/CD, data refresh jobs) and define environments for dev/test/production with rollback checkpoints.
  • Agree cost transparency model: each work item sized, estimated, and approved before build.

3. Delivery Streams

  • Systems Development: Agile sprints delivering enhancements, integrations, and UI/UX refinements aligned to Cottsway’s digital priorities.
  • Data & Reporting: Build governed semantic layers, automated KPI packs, and self-service dashboards with versioned documentation.
  • Automation & Support: Monitor scheduled jobs, handle reactive tickets within SLA, and continuously harden performance and security.

4. Governance & Improvement

  • Weekly stand-ups with Cottsway PMO for prioritisation and capacity balancing.
  • Monthly service reviews: SLA metrics, cost-to-value analysis, risk & dependency log, sustainability and ethical supply-chain updates.
  • Quarterly roadmap refresh feeding into the 24-month plan and informing the optional 12-month extension decision point.

Service-Level Commitments

  • Incident response: P1 acknowledgement within 15 minutes, fix or workaround within 4 hours; lower tiers scaled accordingly.
  • Change delivery: Approved scope estimated in ≤2 working days; sprint execution tracked end-to-end in shared tooling.
  • Reporting cadence: Core KPI packs refreshed nightly; regulatory/board packs validated at least 3 days before submission deadlines.
  • Transparency: Real-time burn-up charts, detailed invoices tied to milestones, and open-book costing.

Ethical & Sustainable Practice

  • Cloud resources right-sized with continuous carbon footprint reporting.
  • Supplier diversity goals baked into every project plan with quarterly impact statements.
  • Data privacy-by-design reviews on all automations, aligned to UK GDPR and Cottsway policies.

Next Steps
